Competition

Autonomy platforms exist, but WaveShift is purpose-built for port monitoring.

Existing Competition

Autonomous Surface Vessel (ASV) Platform Builders
e.g., Saronic, Saildrone, OceanAlpha

Strengths

  • Proven autonomy stack
  • Hardware platforms in production

Limitations vs WaveShift

  • Generalist platforms, not port-specific
  • Focus on vessel sales, not monitoring services
Maritime Autonomy & Defense Integrators
e.g., Sea Machines Robotics, L3Harris, HavocAI

Strengths

  • High-performance autonomy
  • Strong defense relationships

Limitations vs WaveShift

  • Defense-first, long procurement cycles
  • Over-specified for commercial ports
Traditional Port Monitoring Systems
Fixed cameras, AIS tracking, crewed patrols

Strengths

  • Proven, well-understood
  • Low upfront complexity

Limitations vs WaveShift

  • Static/shift-based coverage
  • High labor costs, blind spots
