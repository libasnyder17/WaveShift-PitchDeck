Autonomous surface vessel built for continuous maritime monitoring

  • Operates continuously without onboard crew
  • Real-time visual + thermal data to a remote operator

  • 24/7 sensing: visual + thermal, day/night operations
  • Autonomous navigation: waypoint-based patrols in defined areas

  • Monitoring-first architecture: persistent awareness without crew risk

Why This Is Different

  • Replaces costly crewed patrol coverage with autonomous persistence
  • Extends beyond fixed cameras limited to line-of-sight
  • Delivers continuous presence without shift limits or fatigue
