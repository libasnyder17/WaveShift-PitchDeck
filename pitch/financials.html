Financial Projections

Judging Prompts

  • • Units sold and unit price
  • • Profit or loss
  • • Cash flow and funding required
  • Unit COGS: Target ~$30-50K per vessel (hardware, assembly, testing)
  • Annual contract value: $150-250K per site (base coverage + expansion)
  • Gross margin target: 60-70% after pilot phase and scale
  • Year 1: Prototype build + demonstration (capital requirement: $200-300K)
  • Year 2: 2-3 paid pilots → first annual contracts (~$300-500K ARR)
  • Year 3+: Scale to 10-15 deployed sites, multiple vessels per site

Revenue Growth Path

Year 1: Development + demonstrations (pre-revenue)

Year 2: Pilots → first contracts (ARR: $300-500K)

Year 3: Expand deployments + fleet per site (ARR: $1.5-3M)

Year 4: Multi-site expansion, proven track record (ARR: $5M+)

Conservative trajectory based on pilot-to-contract conversion and fleet expansion
